DISCOVER MEGHALAYA:

Welcome to Meghalaya,
a state renowned for its lush green landscapes, waterfalls, and vibrant cultures.
Located in the northeastern part of India, Meghalaya offers a unique blend of natural beauty and traditional charm.
From the stunning waterfalls of Cherrapunji to the serene landscapes of Shillong, Meghalaya is a paradise for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

VISIT WITH US

Shillong: The capital city, known for its beautiful lakes like Umiam Lake, vibrant markets, and as a gateway to the northeastern region.
Cherrapunji: Famous for its heavy rainfall, lush greenery, and stunning waterfalls such as Nohkalikai Falls and Seven Sisters Falls.
Mawlynnong: Renowned as one of the cleanest villages in Asia, known for its picturesque beauty and the living root bridges.
Jowai: Known for its scenic landscapes, including the beautiful Nartiang Monoliths and serene river views.
Umngot River: Famous for its crystal-clear waters and scenic boat rides, offering a unique experience in Dawki.
