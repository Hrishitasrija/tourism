DISCOVER WEST BENGAL:

Welcome to West Bengal,
a state rich in culture, history, and natural beauty.
Located in the eastern part of India, West Bengal boasts a vibrant blend of tradition and modernity.
From the bustling cityscape of Kolkata to the serene beauty of Darjeeling’s tea gardens, West Bengal offers an array of experiences that captivate every traveler’s senses.

VISIT WITH US

Kolkata: The vibrant capital city, known for its colonial architecture, cultural festivals, and historic landmarks like the Victoria Memorial and Howrah Bridge.
Darjeeling: Famous for its tea gardens, stunning views of the Himalayas, and the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
Sundarbans: Home to the largest tidal mangrove forest in the world and the Bengal tiger, offering unique wildlife and eco-tourism experiences.
Shantiniketan: Founded by Rabindranath Tagore, this town is known for its educational institutions and cultural festivals, including the famous Poush Mela.
Kalimpong: A picturesque hill station with beautiful monasteries, vibrant flower nurseries, and panoramic views of the Kanchenjunga range.
Murarai: Known for its ancient temples, serene rural landscapes, and traditional Bengali culture, offering a glimpse into local life away from the city bustle.
Purulia: Famous for its folk arts, scenic landscapes, and the beautiful Bamni Falls, which provide a peaceful retreat into nature.
