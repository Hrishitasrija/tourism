DISCOVER KASHMIR:

Welcome to Kashmir,
a land of breathtaking landscapes, lush gardens, and serene lakes.
Located in the northern part of India, Kashmir is renowned for its natural beauty, with snow-capped mountains and tranquil valleys.
From the picturesque Dal Lake in Srinagar to the adventure-filled slopes of Gulmarg, Kashmir offers a unique and unforgettable experience.

VISIT WITH US

Srinagar: Known for its beautiful Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens, and traditional houseboats offering a unique stay experience.
Gulmarg: Famous for its lush green meadows and as a premier destination for skiing and snowboarding in the winter.
Pahalgam: A serene town known for its lush landscapes, starting point for the Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage, and beautiful river views.
Sonamarg: Renowned for its stunning meadows, Thajiwas Glacier, and opportunities for trekking and trout fishing.
Yusmarg: Known for its scenic beauty and tranquil environment, perfect for a peaceful getaway amidst lush meadows and pine forests.
