DISCOVER RAJASTHAN:

Welcome to Rajasthan,
a state renowned for its majestic forts, vibrant culture, and stunning deserts.
Located in the northwestern part of India, Rajasthan is a land of regal history and architectural wonders.
From the grandeur of Jaipur's palaces to the serene beauty of Udaipur's lakes, Rajasthan offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

VISIT WITH US

Jaipur: The capital city known for its splendid forts like Amber Fort, City Palace, and the iconic Hawa Mahal.
Udaipur: Famous for its beautiful lakes, including Lake Pichola, and majestic palaces like the City Palace and Jag Mandir.
Jaisalmer: Known as the "Golden City," with its stunning sandstone architecture, including the Jaisalmer Fort and the havelis of Patwon Ki Haveli.
Jodhpur: The "Blue City," renowned for the impressive Mehrangarh Fort and the vibrant blue-painted houses in the old city.
Ranthambore: A popular wildlife sanctuary known for its tiger population and the historic Ranthambore Fort.
Pushkar: Famous for its sacred lake, the Brahma Temple, and the annual Pushkar Camel Fair, a vibrant cultural event.
Mount Abu: The only hill station in Rajasthan, known for the serene Dilwara Temples and the picturesque Nakki Lake.
